Reinforcement Learning with a Ball and Beam System

Welcome to our interactive learning platform! This simulation demonstrates how reinforcement learning can be applied to control a ball and beam system. The goal is to train an agent to balance a ball at a target position by adjusting the beam's angle. Use the tabs below to explore different modes and view training results.

Manual Mode

Control the ball and beam system manually to understand the dynamics of the simulation.

New Training

Select different observation spaces and train the reinforcement learning model with new data.

View All Results

View the results of all your simulations and training sessions to analyze performance.
